# Agent

## [OpenClaw](https://openclaw.ai/)
An open-source, locally-operated AI agent framework that acts as an execution engine, enabling artificial intelligence to understand natural language and directly operate your computer to perform real-world tasks—such as managing emails or automating workflows—rather than merely generating text suggestions.

## [Manus](https://manus.im/)
A general-purpose AI agent that doesn't just provide ideas—it autonomously executes complex, multi-step tasks and delivers complete results.
