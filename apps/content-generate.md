# Content Generate

## Image creation

### [Nano Banana Pro]()
A professional AI image generation and editing model developed by Google DeepMind, built on the Gemini 3 Pro architecture, that enables high-fidelity 4K image creation, precise multilingual text rendering, and advanced editing capabilities such as multi-image fusion and consistent character generation across scenes.

### [Midjourney]()
An independent research lab that develops an AI-powered image generation tool capable of creating high-quality, artistic visuals from natural language text prompts.

### [Stable diffusion]()
An open-source deep learning model that generates high-quality images from text descriptions (text-to-image) by iteratively denoising random noise through a diffusion process.

## Video creation

### [Sora]()
A generative AI model developed by OpenAI that creates realistic and imaginative videos up to 60 seconds long from text descriptions.
