# AI Search

## [Perplexity AI](https://www.perplexity.ai/)

An AI-powered conversational search engine that provides direct answers with cited sources, combining the capabilities of ChatGPT with real-time web search.

## [Microsoft Copilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com/)
A free AI-powered conversational assistant that combines OpenAI's GPT-4 model with real-time internet search to provide accurate, cited answers and generate content across text and images.
